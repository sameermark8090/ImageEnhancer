Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image enhancer tool

How does the image enhancer work?

Our tool uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your image and intelligently enhance its quality by increasing resolution, reducing noise, and improving sharpness while preserving important details.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, and more. The enhanced image will be provided in the same format as the original unless you choose to convert it.

Is there a limit to image size?

You can enhance images up to 10MB in size. For best results with large enhancement levels (8x), we recommend starting with images at least 500px in width or height.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, we process your images temporarily during enhancement but don't store them permanently. Your images are deleted from our servers shortly after processing.

Is this service really free?

Yes, our basic image enhancement service is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may offer premium features in the future, but core functionality will remain free.
